U.S. Trademark Registration Process

Comprehensive Guide to Federal Trademark Protection

STEP 1
Mark Selection
Choose a distinctive mark (word, phrase, symbol, or design). Fanciful or arbitrary marks offer strongest protection as they are inherently distinctive.
STEP 2
Intake Form & Consultation
Complete intake form and consult with trademark attorney. $100 consultation fee credited toward registration costs if proceeding.
STEP 3
Clearance Search
Comprehensive search of existing trademarks to identify potential conflicts. Typically $800 for one mark in one class.
STEP 4
Opinion Letter
Attorney provides detailed analysis of registration likelihood and potential conflicts based on search results.
STEP 5
Application Preparation & Filing
Prepare and file application with USPTO. Filing fees and legal fees typically $400-500 for one mark in one class.
STEP 6
USPTO Examination
USPTO examining attorney reviews application for compliance and substantive issues. May issue Office Actions requiring response.
STEP 7
Publication & Opposition
Approved applications published in Official Gazette. 30-day opposition period for third parties to challenge registration.
STEP 8
Registration & Maintenance
If no opposition, registration certificate issues within 2 months. Ongoing maintenance filings required to keep registration active.
